Heat recedes at last

Decreasing Heat

August 28 – September 1
← Back to Index

The relentless weight of summer finally begins to lift. Evenings carry a hint of coolness and the first rustle of autumn grasses.

Known as 天地始粛 (Tenchi hajimete samushi), this microseason celebrates the moment when both heaven and earth start to feel refreshed.

Shadows lengthen, cicadas grow quiet, and crickets take over the night shift. Windows open to invite in the breeze that once seemed impossible.

It is a small mercy after months of heat, a promise that longer nights and clearer skies are on their way.

天地始粛

Evening breezes sigh
Heat loosens its heavy grip
Crickets claim the night

– Original haiku
