    This is a copycat recipe that produces a pretty tasty sandwich! Don't forget the pickle juice brine. It's essential!

  2. Tamago Kake Gohan

    This egg and rice dish just hits different. Get pasturized eggs if you aren't a fan of raw eggs!

  3. Supermarket Bread

    My go-to bread recipe. What can I say? It just tastes great! (And is super easy!)

  4. Hokkaido Dinner Rolls

    These rolls are savory, salty, soft, and fluffy. Perfection
